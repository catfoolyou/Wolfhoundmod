Wolfhound's Mod is a content/quality of life mod.
Basically a mod that adds random stuff I want in the game.

Content:
Countless items, weapons, bosses and more are added into the game.
Around 200 items,
5 accessories and 8 armor sets,
6 enemies (more to come) and 1 town NPC,
7 bosses (and counting)

QOL changes:
Melee weapons autoswing overhauls,
Minor changes to some guns and bows

Full support for TerrariaOverhaul
Planned support for Boss Checklist

It is still in beta, so use at your own risk.
See the Github for more updates.
